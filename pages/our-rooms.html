OUR ROOMS

SURROUNDED BY HISTORY

IN THE HEART OF THE CITY

Enjoy the unique experience of staying in the historic center of Prizren, surrounded by culture, tradition, and vibrant city life. Our modern and comfortable rooms are filled with natural light and designed to offer a perfect balance of style and relaxation. Each room is thoughtfully equipped with quality furnishings, combining elegance and functionality to ensure a pleasant stay. Located in the old part of the city, every room offers its own charm, with views of Prizren's lively streets or peaceful surroundings. Whether you are visiting for business or leisure, Hotel Centrum provides a welcoming atmosphere where comfort meets authentic local experience. Discover your perfect stay with us and enjoy Prizren from its very heart.
