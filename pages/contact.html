Information

Hotel Centrum is located in the heart of Prizren's historic old town, within walking distance of major attractions including Prizren Castle, the beautiful mosques and churches. Our team is available 24/7 to assist with reservations, inquiries, and any special requests you may have. We look forward to welcoming you to Hotel Centrum Prizren and ensuring your stay is memorable and comfortable.

How to Arrive to Hotel Centrum

From Prizren Bus Station
By Taxi it will take 10 minutes. On Foot it will take approximately 16 minutes.
From Pristina International Airport
The journey takes about 1 hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes by car, depending on traffic.
From Tirana International Airport
It usually takes around 2.5 to 3 hours by car, depending on traffic.
From Skopje International Airport
Takes approximately 2 to 2.5 hours by car, depending on traffic.
