Information

Hotel Centrum is located in the heart of Prizren's historic old town, within walking distance of major attractions including Prizren Castle, the beautiful mosques and churches. Our team is available 24/7 to assist with reservations, inquiries, and any special requests you may have. We look forward to welcoming you to Hotel Centrum Prizren and ensuring your stay is memorable and comfortable.

How to Arrive to Hotel Centrum

By Airport
Pristina International Airport is approximately 90 kilometers away. From the airport, you can arrange a taxi or rental car to reach our hotel in approximately 1.5 hours.
By Train
The nearest railway station is in Prizren, just 5 kilometers away from Hotel Centrum. From the station, local taxis and transportation services are readily available.
By Car
Hotel Centrum is easily accessible by car. We offer parking facilities for our guests. Follow the main roads to Prizren, and our concierge team can provide detailed directions.
