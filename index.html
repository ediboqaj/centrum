THE HOTEL

IN THE HEART OF PRIZREN

Welcome to Hotel Centrum Prizren, the first hotel built to international standards in the city's post-war years. Nestled in the historic old town, just steps from the iconic Prizren Castle and centuries-old mosques and churches, our boutique hotel offers guests a unique connection to history and culture. Family-run since its founding by three brothers, Hotel Centrum blends modern comfort with authentic local charm, ensuring every stay is memorable.
